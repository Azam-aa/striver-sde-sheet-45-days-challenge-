# Pow(x,n)

## Core Trigger

Large power calculation.

---

## Approach

Binary Exponentiation.

If power is odd:

Multiply answer.

Square x every step.

---

## Pattern

Binary Exponentiation

---

## Complexity

TC: O(log N)

SC: O(1)

---

## Memory Trick

Odd?

Multiply

↓

Square

↓

Divide Power By 2

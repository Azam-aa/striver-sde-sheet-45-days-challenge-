# Fractional Knapsack

## Core Trigger

Items can be broken.

Maximize value.

---

## Approach

Sort by value/weight ratio.

Pick highest ratio first.

---

## Pattern

Greedy

---

## Complexity

TC: O(N log N)

SC: O(1)

---

## Memory Trick

Value / Weight

↓

Highest Ratio First

↓

Take Full Or Fraction

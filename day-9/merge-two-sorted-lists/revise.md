# Merge Two Sorted Lists

## Core Trigger

Two sorted linked lists.

Merge into one sorted list.

---

## Approach

Dummy Node.

Compare both lists.

Attach smaller node.

---

## Pattern

Two Pointers

Linked List

---

## Complexity

TC: O(N+M)

SC: O(1)

---

## Memory Trick

Compare
↓
Attach Smaller
↓
Move Pointer
